About ZipperOS

ZipperOS is a next-generation Linux distribution focused on performance, customization, and modern usability. It combines powerful built-in apps, recovery tools, and community-driven features.

Users shape the OS: ZipperOS evolves based on requests and ideas submitted by the community. Whether it’s improved touchscreen support, music tools, or advanced recovery, your voice matters.
